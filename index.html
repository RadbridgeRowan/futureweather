Overview

Future Weather is a simple, fast, and integration‑ready platform for generating future climate‑adjusted weather files (EPW, DDY, STAT) for building performance modeling and other planning or simulation tools that require weather file inputs.

Built in partnership with the academic creator of the Future Weather Generator, Dr. Eugénio Rodrigues, our morphing pipeline has been extensively validated against the original tool across thousands of scenarios. It also includes enhanced features such as greater flexibility in selecting future periods, an intuitive web interface, and an API for programmatic access.

If you're modeling a facility that will exist for 20 years or longer then you've come to the right place. Whether you want to optimize costs, reduce liability, comply with regulations, conduct research - or you're just curious about how your facility will perform in the future - incorporating future weather files into your workflow can enhance your outcomes.

How it works

1. Choose location and scenario

Select a location, upload your EPW file, pick a future period, climate scenario, and climate model(s).

2. Generate future weather file

We run your EPW file through our morphing pipeline and incorporate climate data to adjust each variable.

3. Download, store, or integrate

Download your files (EPW/DDY/STAT) immediately or come back and download them later.
